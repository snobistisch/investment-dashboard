# Citrini Research Tracker — Phase 1 research

Built exclusively from public sources: the free tier of the Citrini Research
Substack (citriniresearch.com), public podcast/interview pages, and one
search-indexed @Citrini7 X post. The paid Citrindex product was not accessed or
reconstructed; where a theme's detail is paywalled, the entry says so.

Method: enumerated the full Substack archive via its public API (free vs paid
flag per post), fetched every substantive free post directly, cross-checked
podcast episode pages, and dropped anything without a direct checkable source.
All dates are from the sources themselves (the X post date is decoded from the
tweet's snowflake ID, not estimated).
